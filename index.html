Violent Crime Trend Comparison








Overall, the data shows a clear decline in violent crimes across Malaysia from 2000 to 2022. While rape and bodily injuries peaked around 2010, they have since dropped by 2022. This overall downward trend reflects the likely success of law enforcement, public safety initiatives, and social interventions in reducing crime rates.
